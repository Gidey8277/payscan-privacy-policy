Privacy Policy for PayScan

Effective Date: 14 February 2026

PayScan ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

PayScan does not collect personal financial information.

The app may access:

  • Camera – to scan payment details such as PayBill, Account Number, Send Money, Pochi, Agent, and Store information.
  • Clipboard – to copy scanned payment data for easy pasting.

2. How We Use Information

All scanned data is processed locally on your device. We do not store, share, or transmit your scanned payment details to any server.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Data Security

We prioritize your privacy. All scanning and data processing happens on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

PayScan does not integrate with mobile money providers such as M-Pesa directly. The app only helps users scan and copy payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

PayScan is not intended for children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
